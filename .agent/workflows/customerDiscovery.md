## Step 2: `market_fit_validator` — Validate PMF + Find Buyers

1. Read SKILL.md: `c:\ohm\.agent\skills\market_fit_validator\SKILL.md`
2. Score PMF across 6 dimensions (Problem Clarity, Solution Fit, WTP, Urgency, Alt Weakness, Reach)

### 🚫 KILL GATE

- PMF < 4 → **STOP. Do not build.**
- PMF 4-6 → Pivot or narrow ICP
- PMF > 6 → Proceed to Step 3
