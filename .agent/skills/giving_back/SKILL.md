# 🌍 OHM Giving Back Narrative — Deep Research Skill

## Core Philosophy

OHM is NOT a charity. OHM is a **self-sustaining engine of regeneration** where every transaction, every subscription, and every interaction feeds back into the planet and humanity. The ecosystems with the **best story in GIVING BACK** will win — because purpose-driven companies outperform extractive ones by 14x in customer loyalty (Deloitte 2024) and attract 4.5x more investment (McKinsey 2025).

## The OHM Pledge: Revenue → Impact Pipeline

Every OHM product generates revenue. A **substantial, non-negotiable portion** flows into two funds:

### 1. 🌈 Paradise Fund (Innovation for Humanity)

- **Source**: 5% of ALL ecosystem revenue + 7% Stream platform fee
- **Governance**: DAO-governed — every OHM soul gets a vote
- **Evaluation**: XPollination 6-Dimension scoring (Technology, Health, Planetary, Social Justice, Freedom, Economic Democracy)
- **Minimum Score**: 70% to qualify for community vote
- **Model**: Milestone-based smart contract releases → projects return a portion of revenue → perpetual engine
- **Categories Funded**: Regenerative Tech, Sovereign Infrastructure, Education, Health & Wellbeing, Art & Culture, Planetary Projects
- **Transparency**: Every XOM traceable on Polygon. Public dashboard. No hidden fees.

### 2. 💚 Social Fund (Direct Empowerment)

- **Source**: OHM ecosystem contributions + retreat solidarity spots
- **Philosophy**: "Help that creates dependency is not help — it's another form of control"
- **Anti-Dependency Design**: 6 principles that ensure independence, not reliance
  1. Time-Bounded Support (3-6 months max)
  2. Milestone-Based Smart Contract Releases
  3. Skills & Tools Focus (not cash handouts)
  4. Pay-It-Forward Spiral (graduates become mentors)
  5. Full Blockchain Transparency
  6. Exit Strategy Required
- **Direct-to-Wallet**: 100% of funds reach the beneficiary. No admin fees. No bureaucratic overhead.
- **Tracks**: Sovereign Soul Grants, Retreat Solidarity Spots (20% rule), Community Bridge Grants

### 3. 🐝 XPollination (The Evaluation Engine)

- **Role**: The quality gateway for Paradise Fund projects
- **Framework**: 6 holistic dimensions, 21 sub-criteria
- **Output**: Interactive spider web charts showing comparative analysis
- **Impact**: Ensures only projects that genuinely serve humanity receive funding
