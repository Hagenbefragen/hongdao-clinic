# 📄 Legal Side Letter Generator (Internal Sovereign Structures)

> **Purpose:** Create robust internal legal documents ("Side Letters") that secure the Founder's operational control and vision while using external board members ("Straw Men") for regulatory compliance.

## 1. Core Document Types

### A. Generalhandlungsvollmacht (General Power of Attorney)

**Goal:** Gives the Founder/Manager full external representation power without being on the public register (ZVR).

- **Scope:** Unlimited (contracts, bank accounts, hiring).
- **Irrevocability:** Must be stated (with "important cause" exception).
- **Validity:** Post-mortal (valid beyond death of signers).

### B. Treuhandvereinbarung (Trust Agreement)

**Goal:** Defines the internal relationship. The Board holds the "Office" in trust for the Founder.

- **Instruction Right:** Founder instructs Board on strategic decisions.
- **Asset Ownership:** Board confirms it holds assets for the Verein/Founder's vision, not personal gain.

### C. Undated Resignation Letters (Schubladenlösung)

**Goal:** Ultimate security mechanism.

- **Content:** "I hereby resign from my position as [Title] effective immediately."
- **Storage:** Held physically or digtally by the Founder.
- **Trigger:** Founder allows dating and submission if Board goes rogue.

## 2. Drafting Checklist

When generating these documents, ensure:

1.  **Clear Parties:** Define "Verein" (Trustee) vs. "Manager" (Trustor).
2.  **Explicit Authority:** List specific powers (Bank, Tax, Court).
3.  **Conflict Clauses:** Manager is exempt from § 181 BGB equivalent (Selbstkontrahieren allowed).
4.  **Survival Clause:** Valid even if Board changes (binds successors).
5.  **Applicable Law:** Austrian Law (ABGB, VerG).

## 3. Usage Instructions

To generate a specific document, use the following prompt structure:

```markdown
**Document Type:** [Generalvollmacht | Treuhandvertrag | Rücktritt]
**Association Name:** OHM RESEARCH
**Board Members:** [Names of Pres/Vice]
**Manager/Founder:** [Name]
**Special Conditions:** [e.g., specific bank access, irrevocable for 10 years]
```

## 4. Legal Disclaimer

> ⚠️ **Internal Use Only:** These documents create a "Shadow Director" structure. While common in holding structures, they can entail personal liability for the "Shadow Director" (Faktischer Geschäftsführer). The Founder must be aware that they are liable as if they were the official organ.
