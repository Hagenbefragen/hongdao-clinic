我们相信，比知识更早萌芽的，是一个孩子感知世界的能力、安顿情绪的力量、自由表达的勇气。
“五音小精灵”正为此而来——这是一场中医艺术体验活动，用声音开启感官，用本草连接自然，用中医智慧贯穿始终。在德国音疗师与执业中医师的共同陪伴下，孩子的专注力、感知力、创造力，将在声音与色彩中悄悄生长。

01 音乐·音声疗愈：
孩子们将亲手体验来自世界各地的疗愈乐器：德国的音锣、尼泊尔的颂钵、法国的音叉、印度的手碟、南美的双音笛、墨西哥的音鼓等。
德国音疗师带来国际前沿的音声疗愈方法，全程中英文双语引导。专业中医师用孩子们听得懂的语言，传递“五音入五脏”的千年智慧，讲述住在肝心脾肺肾里的“角徵宫商羽”五位小精灵的故事。

02 绘画·本草力量：
中医师带领孩子们认识常见中药材，观察形状、触摸纹理、嗅闻气味。孩子们用画笔描绘属于自己的“本草图鉴”，释放自由的表达。

## English Translation
Five-Note Melody Elves (Sound Healing & Art for Children)
Location: Shenzhen Hongdao TCM Clinic, Nanshan Medical Device Industry Park, Shenzhen, China
Time: April 18th at 10:00 AM / April 19th at 3:00 PM

We believe that what sprouts earlier than knowledge is a child's ability to perceive the world, the power to settle emotions, and the courage to express themselves freely.
"Five-Note Melody Elves" is here for this—an art experience workshop combining sound and Chinese herbs. Facilitated by a German sound therapist and a licensed TCM doctor, children's focus, perception, and creativity grow naturally.

01 Sound Healing & Instruments:
Children get to experience healing instruments from all over the world: German gongs, Nepalese singing bowls, French tuning forks, Indian handpans, South American double-tone flutes, and Mexican drums.
The German sound therapist guides the session in English and Chinese. The TCM doctor explains the ancient wisdom of "Five Notes entering Five Organs" through stories of five elves representing Jue, Zhi, Gong, Shang, and Yu living in the liver, heart, spleen, lungs, and kidneys.

02 Painting & Herb Art:
TCM doctors introduce common herbs to children by shape, texture, and scent. Children paint their own "Herbal Illustrations," establishing a connection with nature.

03 Details:
- Target Group: Children aged 3–10 (one parent can accompany)
- Group Size: Max 7 families per session
- Fee: 199 RMB
- Facilitators:
  * Deng Nanjing (International Sound Therapist, founder of Lebensfluss TCM Clinic in Germany)
  * Dr. Jingwen Qiao (Licensed TCM Physician, Doctor from Fudan University)
